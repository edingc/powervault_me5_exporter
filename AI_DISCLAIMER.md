# AI Disclaimer

Portions of this repository were generated by AI tools. All code was reviewed by a human before inclusion.

This project started as a way to explore AI tools and better understand how to leverage them in my daily work. The project ended up fulfilling a real need for better monitoring of a Dell PowerVault storage appliance. I believe it highlights one of AI's actual strengths, which is taking structured data and presenting it in different ways.

Most of the AI use went toward eliminating repetitive boilerplate. For example, mapping raw API JSON data to Go structures without endless copy-paste. 

There were numerous models involved in the process, as I often found it best to "rubber duck" ideas against multiple models and myself to find a good solution.

To be clear, while I have exposure to and experience with programming, I am _not_ a programmer. That highlight's the biggest win of this project: something that would have taken weeks of solid development was up and functional in just a few days.
